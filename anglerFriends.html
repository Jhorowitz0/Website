Angler Friends - WORK IN PROGRESS

Angler Friends is a social space where players navigate the world as angler fish, illuminating it with emotion. Following the natural gender dynamics in angler fish, if players chose female, the game will use their webcam to read their facial expression. This will manifest as a colored light the player can use to illuminate and interact with the world. Male angler fish, however, have no light and instead seek out females to attach themselves to.
